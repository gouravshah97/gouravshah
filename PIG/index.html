1. Roll the dice and the value will be added to your Round Score.

2. If you roll 1 you lose your round score and the turn is passed to the other player.

3. You can use the 'Save And Pass' option to add your round score to your total score and pass your turn.

4. First player to reach 100 total score win.

5. Read More : https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pig_(dice_game)

6. There are 2 power-ups that each player can use only one time

  A. GAMBLE - Roll an even number and 5 times of that value will be added to your round score. Roll an odd number and lose 3 times of that value from your round score. Rolling 1 will just end your turn and pass the round.

  B. SWAP - This power will automatically roll a new dice. If you get 6, your score will be swapped with your opponent.
